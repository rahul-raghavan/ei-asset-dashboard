# EI ASSET Skills Analysis Report: Grades 3-7
## PEP School V2 | January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis identifies both **program strengths** to maintain and **foundational skill gaps** that persist across grade levels. The findings support a move toward mastery-based learning by identifying specific skills that need remediation at each level.

**Program Strengths (What's Working Well):**
1. Grammar and vocabulary instruction consistently strong - vocabulary peaks at 90% in Grade 6
2. Arithmetic and computational skills solid across grades (73-80%)
3. Grade 6 is a peak performance year - teaching methods here should be studied and replicated
4. Classification and recall skills in Science show good progression

**Key Areas for Improvement:**
1. Spelling/Phonics weak in Grades 3-5 (58%), only fixed by Grade 6
2. Real-world math applications critically weak (48%) in Grade 3
3. Scientific explanation skills weak across ALL elementary grades (44-61%)
4. Sharp performance cliff in Grade 7 across all subjects
5. 15+ students struggling across ALL THREE subjects need holistic intervention

These students consistently struggle with:

Common weak skills among struggling students:
- **Science**: Hypothesis formulation, explaining processes
- **Maths**: Problem solving, advanced applications

## SECTION 7: Recommended Actions

### Immediate Actions (This Term)

#### 1. Grade 3 Math Intervention - PRIORITY
- **Focus**: Real-world applications (money, time, measurement)
- **Current**: 48%
- **Target**: 65%+
- **Method**: Daily 15-min practical math activities using real objects
- **Why**: This is the lowest score in the entire school

#### 2. Grades 3-5 Science Writing Program
- **Focus**: "Explain why" and "Predict what happens if"
- **Target**: Scientific explanation skills
- **Method**: Structured writing frames for scientific reasoning
- **Template**: "I think ___ will happen because ___"

#### 3. Grades 3-4 Systematic Phonics Program
- **Focus**: Phonics patterns and spelling rules (not just memorization)
- **Current**: 58% (unchanged Grade 3→4)
- **Target**: 70%+ by end of Grade 4
- **Method**: Daily phonics instruction with decodable texts

#### 4. Individual Learning Plans for 15 Cross-Subject Struggling Students
- Create diagnostic profiles for each student
- Assess for learning differences
- Weekly monitoring and support sessions

### Medium-term Actions (Next 2-3 Months)

#### 5. Grade 6→7 Transition Support
- Pre-teach Grade 7 concepts in Term 3 of Grade 6
- Algebra readiness program before formal algebra
- Bridge course for vocabulary and scientific terminology
- Summer transition program

#### 6. Skill-Based Remedial Groups
- Use the dashboard's Group Analysis feature
- Create cross-grade groups for specific skills:
  - "Problem Solving" group (Grades 3, 4, 7)
  - "Scientific Explanation" group (Grades 3-6)
  - "Spelling & Phonics" group (Grades 3-5)
- 4-week intensive intervention cycles

### Long-term Actions (Curriculum Review)

#### 7. Spiral Curriculum for Persistent Weaknesses
- Ensure spelling/phonics is revisited in Grades 4-5, not assumed mastered
- Build scientific explanation into every science unit (not just end-of-chapter)
- Connect math to real-world contexts in EVERY lesson

#### 8. Mastery Checkpoints
- Don't advance students with <60% in foundational skills
- Create "mastery gates" at end of Grades 3, 5, and 7
- Re-teach and re-assess until mastery achieved

#### 9. Language Across Curriculum Focus
- Train all teachers (not just English) in reading comprehension strategies
- Vocabulary instruction in Science and Math classes
- Comprehension checks before problem-solving
